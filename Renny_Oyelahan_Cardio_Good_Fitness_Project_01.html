  • Education shows a high correlation with fitness, usage, income, miles. This indicates that the more educated a customer is, the more likely they are to be fit, use the treadmill more, and run more miles per week.
  • Education also shows a high correlation with income which is somewhat expected because the more educated a customer is, the more likely they are to earn more.
  • Age shows a high correlation with income. This indicates that the older the customer is, the more money they earn.
  • Usage, Fitness, and miles show a high correlation with each other which is expected. This indicates that the more fit the customer is, the more they use the treadmill which results in more miles ran per week.
  • None of the variables show a negative correlation with each other.

Observations

TM195 customer profile:

  • More customers, regardless of their age, tend to purchase TM195 than customers that purchase TM798.
  • Tends to have between 12-18 years of education.
  • Tends to use the treadmill between 2-5 days per week.
  • On average, tends to rate their fitness level a 3.
  • Tends to earn between 30000 and 69000.
  • Tends to run an average of 90 miles per week.
  • Males and females tend to purchase TM195 at the same rate.
  • Of the Customers that purchase TM195, slightly more of them tend to be Partnered.
  • Tends to be rate themselves as less fit then customers of TM798 and slightly more fit than customers of TM498.
  • Tends to report a similar usage to customers of TM498 and less usage than customers of TM798.
  • Tends to be about as educated as the customers of TM498 and less educated than customers of TM798.

TM498 customer profile:

  • Tends to be about the same age range as customers that purchase TM195, although more customers purchase TM498 than TM195.
  • Tends to have about the same years of education (12-18 years) as customers that purchase TM195.
  • On average, they use the treadmill 3 times per week.
  • Tends to rate their fitness level between 1 to 4.
  • Tends to earn about the same of TM195 customers.
  • Tends to run an average of about 90 miles per week (similar to TM195 cutomer profile).
  • Males and females purchase TM498 about the same.
  • Of the customers that purchase TM498, more of them them to be partnered
  • Tend to rate themselves less fit and use the treadmill less often than customers that purchase TM798.
  • Tend to have similar years in education and miles run per week like customers of TM195.

TM798 custome profile:

  • Of the different age groups, the least number of customers purchased TM798.
  • Tends to have between 14 to 21 years of education.
  • Tends to report using their treadmills between 3 to 7 times per week.
  • Tends to rate their fitness level between 3 to 5.
  • On average, they tend to earn about 79000, which is higher than the maximum earned by customers that purchased TM195 and TM498.
  • Tend to run between 90 and about 320 miles per week, which is higher than those ran by customers that purchased TM195 and TM498.
  • Of the customers that purchased TM798, there are more males than females.
  • Of the customers that purchased TM798, there are more partnered than singles.
  • Tend to report the highest fitness level and run the most miles per week.
  • Tend to use their treadmill the most of the other customer profiles.

Recommendations

  • Customers that purchase TM195 and TM498 share similar characteristics and companies should target customers regardless of their gender and marital staus that are between 19 to 50 years old and earn less than 70000. They also have at most 18 years of education, and are not extreme users of the treadmill

  • For TM798 customers, companies should target males that are in a relationship that have the most years of education, that report they religously use their treadmill, and rate themselves as very fit, and on average earn more and run more miles than the customers that purchased TM195 and TM498
